The Vision

A KIOSK wherever the trip takes you.

We are building toward KIOSK stores, vending machines, and circular clothing racks at airports, nearby hotels, cities, and remote locations — places where people can upgrade their wardrobe on the go.

How it starts

Chains, tees, then the full uniform.

KIOSK starts with simple, testable pieces — chains and tees — then expands into hoodies, sweaters, shirts, pants, accessories, and everyday essentials that elevate a fit for any occasion.

01. Essentials

Clean, wearable basics for travel, errands, nights out, workdays, and last-minute plans.

02. Accessories

Chains, bracelets, and small details that make a simple outfit feel intentional.

03. Experiments

Fast product tests, limited designs, collabs, and drops that keep the brand moving.

Marketplace

Built for rare finds and local stores.

KIOSK will connect thrift stores, independent brands, and remote shop owners with customers looking for unique clothing, accessories, and products. Some pieces will live online. Others will only be available at physical KIOSK locations.

Thrift Stores

Curated secondhand and one-off pieces with better digital reach.

Independent Brands

A place for Shopify-powered brands to plug into a discovery-based marketplace.

Physical Exclusives

Products customers can only buy from a real rack, vending machine, kiosk, or pop-up.

Lost & Found

Treasure-hunt drops with coordinates.

Special KIOSK pieces can be hidden behind coordinates, clues, and remote locations. Customers will have to physically travel, explore, and unlock the item — turning shopping into a reason to move.
